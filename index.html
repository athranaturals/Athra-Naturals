Busting Myths

Common Misconceptions

Myth 1Coconut oil is unhealthy because it is high in saturated fat
Fact
While coconut oil contains saturated fat, it is primarily composed of MCTs metabolized differently than long-chain fats. In moderation, it can be a healthy addition to your diet.
Myth 2Virgin coconut oil is the same as regular coconut oil
Fact
Virgin coconut oil is extracted from fresh coconut meat without heat or chemicals, preserving its nutrients. Regular coconut oil may be refined and lack these benefits entirely.
Myth 3Coconut oil causes weight gain
Fact
When consumed in moderation, coconut oil can support metabolism and energy levels. Like any calorie-dense food, overconsumption may lead to weight gain — but moderate use supports a healthy lifestyle.
Myth 4Coconut oil is only good for cooking
Fact
Beyond the kitchen, coconut oil has numerous applications in skincare, haircare, baby care, and oral health — making it a truly versatile product in your wellness routine.
Myth 5All coconut oil is the same
Fact
There are various types — virgin, refined, and hydrogenated — each with different properties. Virgin coconut oil is the gold standard, preserving maximum nutrients and natural benefits.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is fermented virgin coconut oil and how is it different?
Fermented virgin coconut oil is made by extracting fresh coconut milk and allowing it to naturally ferment and separate over 24 to 48 hours at room temperature — no heat, no chemicals. This cold-process method retains maximum natural nutrients, lauric acid, and antioxidants.
Is ATHRA NATURALS coconut oil safe for cooking?
Yes, absolutely. Our fermented virgin coconut oil has a natural mild coconut aroma and is perfect for cooking, sauteing, and baking. It has a moderate smoke point suitable for everyday Indian cooking.
How should I use it for hair and skin?
For hair: warm a small amount and massage into scalp and lengths. Leave 30 minutes or overnight before washing. For skin: apply a thin layer as a moisturiser, massage oil, or makeup remover. Ideal for sensitive skin too.
Why does the oil sometimes solidify?
This is completely natural and a sign of high purity! Virgin coconut oil solidifies below 24 degrees C and melts above it. Simply place the bottle in warm water to liquefy it — quality is not affected at all.
What is the shelf life and how should I store it?
18 to 24 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Always use a clean dry spoon to avoid moisture contamination.
