Busting Myths

Common Misconceptions

Myth 1Coconut oil is unhealthy because it is high in saturated fat
Fact
Coconut oil is primarily composed of MCTs metabolised differently than long-chain fats. In moderation, it can be a healthy addition to your diet.
Myth 2Virgin coconut oil is the same as regular coconut oil
Fact
Virgin coconut oil is extracted from fresh coconut meat without heat or chemicals, preserving all nutrients. Regular refined oil lacks these benefits entirely.
Myth 3Coconut oil causes weight gain
Fact
In moderation, coconut oil supports metabolism and energy levels. Like any calorie-dense food, overconsumption may cause weight gain — but moderate use is healthy.
Myth 4Coconut oil is only good for cooking
Fact
Beyond the kitchen, coconut oil excels in haircare, baby care, and oral health — a truly versatile wellness product used for thousands of years.
Myth 5All coconut oil is the same
Fact
Virgin, refined, and hydrogenated coconut oils are very different. Virgin coconut oil is the gold standard — preserving maximum nutrients and natural benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is fermented virgin coconut oil and how is it different?
Fermented virgin coconut oil is made by extracting fresh coconut milk and allowing it to naturally ferment and separate — no chemicals. This retains maximum nutrients, lauric acid and antioxidants, far superior to refined coconut oil.
Is ATHRA NATURALS coconut oil safe for cooking?
Yes, absolutely. Our fermented virgin coconut oil has a natural mild coconut aroma, is perfect for cooking, sauteing and baking with a moderate smoke point suitable for everyday Indian cooking.
How should I use it for hair care?
Warm a small amount and massage into scalp and lengths. Leave 30 minutes or overnight before washing. It strengthens hair strands, reduces dandruff and promotes hair growth with regular use.
Why does the oil sometimes solidify?
This is completely natural and a sign of high purity! Virgin coconut oil solidifies below 24°C and melts above it. Simply place the bottle in warm water to liquefy — quality is not affected at all.
What is the shelf life and how should I store it?
18 to 24 months when stored in a cool dry place away from sunlight. Always use a clean dry spoon to avoid moisture contamination and extend its natural life.

Yes! We offer Unrefined Coconut Oil (Cold Pressed and Wood Pressed) and Unrefined Sesame Oil (Cold Pressed and Wood Pressed) — all made from sulphur-free copra and handpicked seeds. Check our Price List section for details.
