Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is fermented virgin coconut oil and how is it different?
Fermented virgin coconut oil is made by extracting fresh coconut milk and allowing it to naturally ferment and separate over 24 to 48 hours at room temperature — no heat, no chemicals. This cold-process method retains maximum natural nutrients, lauric acid, and antioxidants, making it far superior to commercially refined coconut oil which is bleached, deodorised, and processed at high heat.
Is ATHRA NATURALS coconut oil safe for cooking?
Yes, absolutely. Our fermented virgin coconut oil has a natural, mild coconut aroma and is perfect for cooking, sauteing, and baking. It has a moderate smoke point suitable for everyday Indian cooking. Many customers also use it as a finishing oil for salads and smoothies.
How should I use it for hair and skin?
For hair: warm a small amount between your palms and massage into the scalp and lengths. Leave for 30 minutes or overnight before washing. For skin: apply a thin layer directly as a moisturiser, massage oil, or makeup remover. Its high lauric acid content gives it natural antibacterial properties, making it ideal for sensitive skin too.
Why does the oil sometimes solidify?
This is completely natural and actually a sign of high purity! Virgin coconut oil solidifies below 24 degrees C and melts above it. During cooler months it may turn white and solid — this does not affect quality at all. Simply place the bottle in warm water or hold it in your hands to liquefy it.
What is the shelf life and how should I store it?
Our coconut oil has a shelf life of 18 to 24 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The natural antioxidants in cold-processed oil help extend its life without artificial preservatives. Always use a clean, dry spoon to avoid moisture contamination.
